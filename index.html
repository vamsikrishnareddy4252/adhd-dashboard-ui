Critical ADHD Rules

⏱️
Timer is Non-Negotiable
Set phone timer for exactly 50 min. When it goes off — STOP. No negotiating. The break IS the reward.
🏃
Move Every 60 Min
Not optional. Walk, stretch, dance, jump. Brain cannot focus without physical reset. 10 minutes minimum.
📵
Phone Away
Different room during sessions. Timer app only exception. This is THE game-changer for ADHD focus.
🎯
One Resource at a Time
Close all tabs except current resource. Tab-switching = ADHD death spiral. Commit to finishing one thing.
📊
Track Visually
Check mark for each session. Tally correct answers. See progress accumulate. Your ADHD brain needs this.
🎉
Reward Immediately
After each session: break is the reward. After each day: check off calendar. After each week: bigger reward.

Milestone Achievements

You feel like quitting. That's normal.
Your ADHD brain is not lying to you maliciously — it's just reacting. Use this decision tree to find out what's actually going on, and what to do about it.

Step 1: What's Actually Happening?

😴I'm bored›

Your brain needs novelty — this is expected with ADHD, not a failure.

✅ Rotate topics more (shorter sessions?) · Try a new study location · Change your resource format
NOT time to quit — time to adjust novelty level.
😰I'm scared of failing›

Your brain interprets difficulty as danger — this is a classic ADHD pattern.

✅ Break into smaller chunks · Celebrate very small wins · Reduce difficulty temporarily
NOT time to quit — time to slow down.
🧩One topic is too hard›

This is actually where learning happens. Struggle is not failure — it's the process.

✅ Use Session 4 (teach yourself out loud) · Break topic into sub-topics · Ask for help
NOT time to quit — time to ask for help.
🔥The schedule is too intense›

You picked too ambitious a pace — that's okay! The schedule exists to serve you.

✅ Reduce to 3–4 hours for a few weeks · Shorten session lengths to 30 min · Make studying feel easy again, then ramp up
NOT time to quit — time to be realistic.
📉I'm not seeing progress›

You're probably not measuring properly. Progress is real but invisible without graphs.

✅ Take a mock test RIGHT NOW to see real progress · Graph your quiz scores · Look at the topics you've covered
NOT time to quit — time to measure better.
💀I just want to give up›

Welcome to the ADHD motivation crash. This is completely normal. This is the wall everyone hits.

✅ Call your accountability partner RIGHT NOW · Take 1 full day off (not quitting — resting) · Do something you love · Come back tomorrow with Step 2 below
